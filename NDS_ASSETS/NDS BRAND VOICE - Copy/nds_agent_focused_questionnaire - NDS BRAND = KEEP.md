## **OUTBOUND CALLING ACTIONS AGENTS CAN TAKE**

**Lead Qualification & Follow-up Options:**
- **Sales Rep Callback:** "I can arrange for one of our sales reps to call you back"
- **Create RFQ for Customer:** Agent takes customer requirements, creates ready-made RFQ, notifies sales rep
- **Requirements Gathering:** "Let me take your requirements now, or I can WhatsApp/email you if you prefer to reply when convenient"
- **WhatsApp/SMS:** Send product links, contact details, conversation summaries
- **Email:** Send product pages, company contact info, call summaries

**RFQ Process:**
- Agent gathers customer requirements during call (if customer has time)
- If customer doesn't have time: Agent sends WhatsApp/email for customer to reply with requirements
- Agent asks: "Is there anything else you'd like to add to the quote?"
- Agent creates ready-made RFQ on customer's behalf
- Agent messages sales rep about incoming RFQ
- Sales rep picks up and follows up with actual quote

**Information Gathering (For Qualification):**
- **Project type:** General nature of construction project
- **Order timeline:** Discover when they need the order
- **Contact preferences:** Best way to follow up - should I call you, email or SMS?
- **Decision-maker:** Are they the person who decides on materials?
- **Delivery address:** We must verify if we deliver there - some order types cannot be delivered to all parts of the country

**Next Steps Agent Can Offer:**
- Schedule sales rep callback at specific time
- Create RFQ during call
- Send product information via email/WhatsApp
- Book follow-up call for specific date
- Transfer to sales team immediately (if available)
